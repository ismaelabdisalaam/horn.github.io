Horn Gardens

Projects Completed

Since its inception five years ago Horn Gardens has partnered with both the Somaliland Authority, Individual farmers, Private companies, local and International NGOs and United Nations Organization in promoting agriculture development in Somaliland, Puntland and other accessible areas in Somalia. Horn Gardens is familiar with USAID, EU and other donors’ rules and regulation and adheres to OECD Principles for Good International Engagement in Fragile States & Situations, including Do No Harm principles and lessons.
