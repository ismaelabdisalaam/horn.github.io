Horn Gardens

About Us

Horn Gardens is dedicated to successful, sustainable and profitable organic agribusiness both as a leading local model integrated farm, combining dairy, commercial fields and greenhouse cultivation, farmers one stop shop, Trainings & Demo farm and a comprehensive out grower system. Horn Gardens is the sole authorized supplier of Amiran Kenya complete modern greenhouse and drip irrigation kits to small scale and commercial farmers in Somaliland, Puntland and other accessible regions in Somalia. It is backed up by years of profitable agribusiness intervention strategies and have the capacity to supply both individual and groups of farmers throughout the year.

We currently operate on our 55 acres farm situated in Carro Yambo, 30 kilometers west of Hargeisa city where we introduced the adoption of modern Green houses and open field drip irrigation systems. We have planted variety of vegetables that includes spinach, kale, cabbages, tomatoes, onions, Potatoes, eggplants, Cucumbers, capsicum, pineapples and fruits. We have currently put 6 acres under drip irrigation technology and established 8 greenhouses for high quality, high value crops. Our goals include production of high-quality local farm products to sell both wholesale and retail and to add value to our produce for our consumers in Hargeisa and other regions of Somaliland.

Our Vision

Is to be the lead partner in making local agriculture production an alternative viable means of livelihood in this Somali region.

Our Mission

Increase and diversify local food production for ourselves, our clients and partners through adoption and use of modern smart agriculture practices. Incorporating technology and local knowledge
